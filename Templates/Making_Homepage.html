4) Pros and Cons: Fireworks vs. Dream Weaver

Pros for Fireworks

  • Easy to use- can just drag the images
  • Easy to adjust all the images, fonts, texts, etc.
  • Easy to link to google chrome

Cons for Fireworks

  1. Doesn't make the code for you-has nothing to do with coding

Pros for Dream Weaver

  • Writes the codes for you
  • Easier to fix the texts- just need one click when Firework requires two
  • Easy to create shapes- better than fireworks as fireworks is more "stiffer"

Cons for Dream Weaver

  • EXTREMELY HARD TO USE
  • complicated codes are everywhere- hard to find the images
  • Hard to put images in
  • Hard to find the tools you are finding for
  • Hard to adjust anything, especially images
  • Hard to fix prblems

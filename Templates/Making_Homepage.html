4) Starting the prototype:

For the prototype, we had to "slice" parts of the storyboard. When we "slice" the parts, we use fireworks and select the tool button, and choose the slice button. The sliced areas are area were the user can click onto the areas, and can be navigated to a different website.

5) Pros and Cons: Fireworks vs. Dream Weaver

As mentioned before, we used both Fireworks and DreamWeaver to make our webpages. Most of the storyboard, the wireframes, and the basic making of the webpage took place in Fireworks, and the complicated codes, and the mixing and making was done in DreamWeaver. This part of the making homepage page, is about me comparing and contrasting both Fireworks and DreamWeaver.
